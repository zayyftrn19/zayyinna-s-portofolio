WHO IS ZAYYINNA?

Zayyinna is a chemical engineering student at Brawijaya University and a recipient of the 2023 TELADAN Tanoto Foundation scholarship. I am a person who is very often directly involved in activities that rely on public speaking and leadership skills. Specifically, the activities of being a master of ceremonies, moderator, project manager, researcher, and human development.

MY CHARACTER STRENGTHS

HONESTY

I am someone who is honest and easy to trust. This means that I am always given the opportunity to take responsibility for someone else. For example, from lecturers, friends, seniors, or my colleagues.

HOPE

I have high hopes for something that I am working on and also always try as hard as possible to carry out my responsibility. This is also what makes me a person who is quite a perfectionist and optimistic about something.

CURIOSITY

I really like to try new and challenging things. This makes me also adaptable.

KINDNESS

I really like serving others and keeping others motivated. I always feel that my presence should benefit others. However, I am also a person who really likes to be appreciated and understood by others.

ZEST

If I already have a goal, I will try my best to be able to make it happen. My enthusiasm will be high when I want to achieve a target.

Investigate - Enterprising

Based on my interests and talents. I am an investigative, entrepreneurial, and interpersonal person. In fact, I really like playing with ideas and solutions to solve or reveal the facts of a problem. In connection with Investigate, I often carry out activities in the form of research, especially scientific research. Apart from that, I am also an enterprising person. I am a person who likes to lead others, make decisions, and take risks and profits from a project that I am working on. This is also why I am often trusted to be chairman of several events and to handle several projects in lectures and organizations. Having a personality that leads to interpersonal is closely related to investigating and entrepreneurship, which is usually done in teams or groups. With my ability to really like building relationships or interacting with other people, it makes it easier for me to carry out all these projects.

    Highlighted Experiences

    Tanoto Scholars Association Brawijaya

    I have been involved in TSA Brawijaya since 2023. It started with me serving as a staff member in the events department, and currently, I hold the position of head of the project department. This means that my diligence and enthusiasm in TSA Brawijaya have enabled me to continue developing and learning many new things, especially in organizing social projects that align with my interests.

    Lecturer Assistant BPPM FT UB

    At first, I couldn't believe it when my professor contacted me to participate in his research. At that time, I was the only student from the 2022 cohort. Naturally, there were concerns about working with someone more senior than me, but it was precisely that experience that enhanced my teamwork skills and communication abilities. Furthermore, my interest in research-related activities has grown."

    Researcher in YAR-TSRA

    If my previous professor's research was conducted on a national and regional scale, I decided to step out of my comfort zone to enhance my research skills by participating in YAR-TSRA activities, which are on an international scale. This is because the entire event is conducted fully in English, from its workshops to the summit event held in Jakarta, and this activity is organized in collaboration with the international organization UNESCO.

    Pekan Ilmiah Mahasiswa Nasional (PIMNAS) 2023

    It was an extraordinary opportunity to be part of the PKM-KC "Biozafe" team. Additionally, we brought home the gold medal. From this experience, I learned that good teamwork will yield good results as well.

    Guardian Angel Assistant Lead Self TELADAN 2024 Tanoto Foundation

    Delegates in ASEAN Global Youth Learning and Excursions 2024

    I could go abroad not just for vacation but to participate in various activities that were very beneficial for both myself and others because this program included class sessions and volunteering. Additionally, I had the opportunity to travel abroad to Malaysia, Singapore, and Thailand with sponsorship provided by the Tanoto Foundation after I submitted a sponsorship proposal earlier.
